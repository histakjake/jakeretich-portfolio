About Me

I'm Jake — a short-form video editor who's spent the last decade learning exactly where an audience's attention goes, and how to keep it there. I've edited behind the scenes for Zach King and done main-channel short-form vertical editing for Mark Rober. My work has generated over half a billion views across platforms, including a single project that crossed 250M.

My background spans two degrees — Youth Ministry and Digital Marketing — and years of producing weekly creative content for a live audience at my church. That combination gives me something most editors don't have: pacing instincts built from holding a real room's attention, not just an algorithm's.
